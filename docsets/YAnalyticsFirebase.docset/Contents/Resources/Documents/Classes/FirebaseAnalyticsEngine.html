  • init(configuration:)

    Initialize Firebase Engine.

    Although technically you can declare multiple instances of FirebaseAnalyticsEngine, it will only call FirebaseApp.configure() once. Therefore we recommend that you only declare a single instance of the FirebaseAnalyticsEngine (but not a singleton!), and you probably want to do so at app launch and have its lifecycle map that of your application.
